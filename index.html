IMPACT ISN'T PHILANTHROPY.

OPPORTUNITY IN EUROPEAN TECH.

Born in Finland, raised in New York, Vienna and London. Now based in The Hague. I invest at 4impact capital, a software-focused impact VC backing pre-seed to Series A founders across the Nordics, DACH and Benelux.

The working thesis: the next generation of European category winners will be impact companies by default, not by label. Finding them early is the job. Writing about it keeps me honest.

I spend a lot of time in the Nordics, where I think founders have historically been too humble about what they're building. I write and speak about that, along with energy tech, European competitiveness, and what it takes to raise capital as an early-stage founder.

Off the clock: value investing the slow way, journaling, building side projects, and the occasional hackathon.
